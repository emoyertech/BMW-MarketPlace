## Core SQL Tables
1. **Users** - User information and settings.
2. **Vehicles** - Details of listed vehicles.
3. **Listings** - Active listings and their statuses.
4. **Service_History** - Maintenance records associated with vehicles.
5. **Photos** - Images related to listings.
6. **Inquiries** - Buyer inquiries stored for tracking.
7. **Messages** - Conversation records between buyers and sellers.
8. **Reviews** - User-generated reviews and ratings.
9. **Transactions** - Record of completed sales.
10. **Saved_Vehicles** - List of vehicles saved by users for later viewing.
